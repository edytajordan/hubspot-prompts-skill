# Sales: Prospecting, Sales Enablement

## Use Cases
Prospecting, Sales Enablement

## Prompt
Draft a follow-up email for [PERSON] about [TOPIC] from our meeting on [DATE]. Use a [TONE: urgent/friendly/formal] tone. Include: 1) Quick meeting recap, 2) Specific next steps we agreed on, 3) Any decisions needed from them, and 4) Suggested next meeting time. Make it concise and action-oriented.

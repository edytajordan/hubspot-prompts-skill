# Sales: Sales Enablement, Optimize Sales Performance, Quote Performance

## Use Cases
Sales Enablement, Optimize Sales Performance, Quote Performance

## Prompt
Analyze all quotes from the last 6 months, including line item details and final quote values. Compare quote acceptance rates by product, discount levels, and quote complexity (number of line items). Identify which product combinations in quotes have the highest close rates.
